ES的配置

三个配置文件：

  • elasticsearch.yml：elasticsearch的相关配置
  • jvm.options：elasticsearch运行的jvm相关设置
  • log4j2.properties elasticsearch日志的配置

配置文件的默认路径： /home/yfs/software/elasticsearch-7.13.4/config

可通过全局变量ES_PATH_CONF指定其他路径中的配置文件启动elasticsearch。

1.Check your cluster, node, and index health, status, and statistics

从中可以看到cluster的运行状态为‘green’。

  • Green - everything is good (cluster is fully functional)
  • Yellow - all data is available but some replicas are not yet allocated (cluster is fully functional)
  • Red - some data is not available for whatever reason (cluster is partially functional)

当cluster为红色时，可用的shards仍可以相应搜索请求。
